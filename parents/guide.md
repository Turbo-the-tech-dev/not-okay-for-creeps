## How to Talk to Your Kids

### Age 4-7
- Use proper names for body parts
- Teach "good touch vs. bad touch"
- Practice saying "NO" loudly
- Identify 3 trusted adults

### Age 8-12
- Discuss online safety
- Review social media boundaries
- Talk about grooming tactics
- Establish open communication

### Age 13+
- Discuss consent explicitly
- Review dating safety
- Talk about reporting mechanisms
- Maintain trust

## Red Flags to Watch For
- Adults who prefer kids' company over peers
- Gifts without your knowledge
- Requests for alone time
- Secretive communication
- Sharing inappropriate content
